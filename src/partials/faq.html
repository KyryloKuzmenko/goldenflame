• Is Golden Flame really free to play?

    Yes! Golden Flame: Co-op Puzzle Adventure is completely free to play. There are no hidden fees, no pay-to-win systems, and no locked levels behind a paywall. We believe in delivering a fully immersive puzzle experience for everyone. You can enjoy the entire adventure from start to finish without spending a single cent. The only thing you’ll need is your brain and maybe a partner! Future expansions may be released, but the core game will always remain free.

  • Can I play solo, or do I need a second player?

    You can absolutely play solo. Golden Flame was designed to work perfectly in both co-op and single-player modes. When playing solo, you’ll alternate control between Aru and Kai using a simple swap mechanic. The puzzles are balanced so that both styles of play offer a smooth and rewarding experience. However, solving puzzles with a friend may bring an extra layer of fun, laughter, and maybe even a few good-natured arguments!

  • Is this a browser game or do I need to download anything?

    Golden Flame runs directly in your browser — no downloads required. It’s built using modern web technologies and optimized for seamless gameplay across most devices. You just click “Play,” and you’re instantly transported into the Flame Temple. All saves are handled in-browser or via your player account (optional). If you choose to play on mobile, everything runs just as smoothly!

  • What platforms is the game available on?

    Golden Flame is currently available on PC, Mac, tablets, and most smartphones through your browser. Whether you're using Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge, the game is fully supported. We’re also working on controller support for those who want that console-like feel. A downloadable version might be released in the future if there’s demand. For now, all you need is an internet connection and a curiosity for fire-powered puzzles.

  • How long does it take to complete the game?

    A typical run of the game takes about 2–3 hours to complete, depending on how quickly you solve the puzzles. Some players breeze through with fast teamwork, while others take their time exploring every hidden corner of the temple. There are optional lore collectibles and alternate endings that can add replay value. We also include a time trial mode if you're into speedrunning. Expect more content in future updates as well.

  • Can I invite a friend who’s never played games before?

    Absolutely! Golden Flame is a great introduction to gaming for new players. The controls are simple, and the puzzle mechanics are intuitive without being overly complicated. We include helpful tooltips, visual cues, and beginner-friendly tutorials at the start. Plus, playing with a patient partner makes it an even better bonding experience. Many of our players say they introduced their friends, partners, or family to games through Golden Flame.

  • Is the game suitable for kids or families?

    Yes, Golden Flame is family-friendly and safe for players of all ages. The game does not include any violence, gore, or inappropriate content. It focuses on critical thinking, teamwork, and exploration. Many families play the game together, with kids solving puzzles and adults helping out with logic-heavy sections. That said, younger children may need assistance with more advanced puzzle levels.

  • What kind of puzzles are in the game?

    The puzzles in Golden Flame range from light-based mechanics like mirror redirection to cooperative lever pulls, flame sequencing, torch relays, and environmental navigation. Some puzzles are time-based, requiring coordination between both players, while others are logic-driven or spatial. No two puzzles are exactly the same, and new mechanics are introduced gradually to keep things fresh. We’ve designed the difficulty curve to be gentle, with plenty of hints built into the environment.

  • Is there a story or just puzzles?

    Golden Flame is more than just a puzzle game — it’s a narrative adventure. As you explore the temple, you’ll uncover the ancient history of the Golden Flame and the civilization that once protected it. Through glowing glyphs, character banter, and secret lore rooms, you piece together a story of sacrifice, magic, and hope. The game features multiple endings depending on how much of the story you uncover. It’s subtle but deeply rewarding for players who love a bit of mystery.

  • Can I save my progress and return later?

    Yes, your progress is automatically saved as you play. Whether you're playing alone or with a friend, the game remembers your puzzle states and checkpoints. You can return at any time and continue right where you left off. If you play with a friend using a shared link, your session can be resumed as long as both players use the same devices. Account creation is optional but allows for cloud saves across devices.

    Yes! We plan to release seasonal events, new puzzle zones, and possibly a sequel depending on player interest. We’re also working on community features like leaderboards, challenge modes, and maybe even a level editor. We love feedback and are listening closely to our player community. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated. The temple may be ancient, but its future is just heating up!
