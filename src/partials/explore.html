A Dungeon That Burns With Personality

Dive into a mysterious ancient temple filled with glowing runes, molten puzzles, and dormant secrets. Each level offers new challenges and visual variety—from stone-carved vaults to glowing lava cathedrals.

Temple Zones:

  • •

    Ember Gate – Tutorial-friendly but not without traps.

  • •

    Mirror Hall – Reflective chaos and logic loops.

  • •

    Lava Gauntlet – Timing-heavy platform sections.

  • •

    Flame Cathedral – Cooperative torch relay puzzles.

  • •

    Inferno Core – The final test of trust and timing.

The temple layout subtly changes every time you play.
